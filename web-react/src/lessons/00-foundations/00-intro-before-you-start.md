# Before You Start, Read This First

Let me be straight with you, because that is the whole point of this class.

This is the other thing. This is the complete class on how to actually do it, properly, from the ground up, so you understand *why* every step works and not just which button to press.

I am not going to fake results for you. I am not going to show you cherry-picked highlights and hide the failures. I am not part of the gimmick economy that has swallowed this whole space right now. I have been doing this for a long time, and I am confident enough in what I am putting in front of you that I will back every claim with my own material: the real workflows, the old techniques, the things that actually hold up.

Here is something most people do not realize until they are deep in it: **the knowledge is all linked together.** You cannot truly master character creation without understanding how the models work, and once you understand how the models work, you understand a huge amount about how AI works in general. So if you commit to this, you are not just walking away able to make consistent characters. You are walking away with a real, structural understanding of the whole field. That is not a bonus I am tacking on. It is just what happens when you learn it the right way instead of the shortcut way.

What I am handing you is the fast track to something that cost me, and a lot of us who built what is available today, thousands of hours to figure out. Every dead end, every wasted dataset, every model that fought back, every workflow that took a hundred tries to get right. You get to skip all of that. Not just the techniques, but the deeper logic behind the workflows, and a lot of other pieces you do not even know to ask about yet. They are all in here.

I will also work with people one-on-one to help untangle the specific kinks and details in their own work, because I will not lie to you about this part either: to get genuinely good, *the best*, you have to understand all of it. There is no version of mastery that skips the understanding.

And if you would rather do it the cheap and easy way? I am not going to stop you. Nano Banana is right there. Go have fun.
